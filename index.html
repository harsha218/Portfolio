About

I am a hardworking individual with a relentless drive to achieve excellence in all that I do. With a dependable nature, I consistently deliver results that exceed expectations. My ability to quickly grasp new concepts and adapt to changing environments sets me apart.

In 2019, I landed my first job at Infosys, where I had the privilege of training on the MERN stack. I consider myself fortunate to have been placed in a project with exceptional mentors, from whom I learned to develop small-scale React applications. Over the course of two years, I honed my skills in building various UI applications.

My journey took an exciting turn when I joined Sureify, a company that quickly felt like family. Starting as a frontend developer, I soon seized the opportunity to broaden my expertise into backend development. For the next two years, I served as a full-stack developer, playing a key role in delivering Sureify's Lifetime Acquire Product to a major insurance client.
